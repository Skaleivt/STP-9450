Ezoterium’s universe consists of multiple beautifully crafted planets, each offering a new visual theme and unique puzzle challenges. From mystical enchanted forests filled with glowing crystals to futuristic alien landscapes alive with advanced technology, every world presents fresh gameplay elements. As you collect stars by completing levels, you unlock new characters and worlds, expanding your adventure across the galaxy. Each planet comes with its own set of puzzles, obstacles, and power-ups, keeping the experience varied and exciting. Progression is rewarding and designed to encourage exploration — the deeper you go, the more mysteries and bonuses you’ll discover. Your puzzle-solving skills will grow as you navigate these diverse environments.
