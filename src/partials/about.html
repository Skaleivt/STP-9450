About Ezoterium

A Match-3 RPG Like No Other
Ezoterium is more than just a match-3 puzzle game — it’s an immersive RPG experience that challenges your strategic thinking and reflexes. Each level is designed to test your problem-solving skills with increasing complexity and engaging mechanics. Unlike typical match-3 games, Ezoterium incorporates character progression and exploration elements. As you complete puzzles, you gain stars that unlock new characters with unique abilities, adding depth and personalization to your gameplay. Explore an expansive universe where each planet has its own lore, puzzles, and secrets waiting to be uncovered. This game blends relaxation with challenge, making it suitable for both casual players and dedicated puzzle enthusiasts.
